Books

Laga Pujangga by Osman Deen

The first book that I will talk about is Laga Pujangga. It was about a teenager named Adwa that was accepted to Academy of Arts (AKSARA) to unravel the mystery of his mother's disappearance. In AKSARA, they have a poem contest called Liga Laga Pujangga. In this league, participant compete in poetry that they learn which embody visual imagery and physical effects.

As someone who loves magics and fantasies, this book does meet my expectations. When reading this, I feel like entering the Harry Potter's world where magic and spell is possible, except this book is even more Interesting as it used classical Malay language for the poem. The detailing for each magics and how the poem related to it was interesting and I enjoy reading it. 9/10!

Atuk by Ebriza Aminnudin

It all started when a group of paranormal received a letter that was asking them to go to an abandoned old folks home. The adventure and mystery starts there, especially after they enter a world that was traumatizing to all of them. The characters have to untangle the mystery on what is going on there and what happened to all of the elderlies.

I read this when I was 13 and I was enjoying it so much. As much as I can remember, the characters had to face the torture like the elderlies did and most of it were disturbing. The characters were facing disruption of those spirit that were persecuted. What makes me like this book more is because of the plot twist that I never see coming. It turns out that one of the members in the group was the one who sent the letter, and he is also one of the spirit. He is the 'Atuk'. 8.5/10!

Saga Empayar Kristal: Armada Azur by Firdaus Ariff

This book is the sequel for Sayap Adinila where the timeline between each story was only a year, if I am not mistaken. It tells about how the Angkor Kingdom trying to conquer Azur Island for its crystal and how the other empire were trying to defeat it. Along the way, death and sadness accompanying them for the sake of peace.

I remember crying when the character named Emrullah died because he just got married at that time. They were going on a war between empire to protect their kingdom. I do not remember much because I have read this a long time ago that the existence of the book is also unknown. But what attract my attention is how crystal was utilised in this story and how the background was set and inspired by Angkor Wat. At the back of the book, the author was literally showing his journey to Angkor Wat and how he planned the story along with some illustration 10/10!.

Movies and Series

Arcane

Arcane is an animated series from the multiplayer online game League of Legends. It was a story about two group of citizens with different level from different places, Piltover and Zaun. The invention of Hextech crystal by Jayce and Viktor have made this story developed and not just focusing on the citizens' condition.

I love this type of art style so much with colourful aesthetic. Arcane also applied different universe and timeline concept which makes the audience wondering different possibilities. It was packed with action and emotions, along with background songs that was majestic and unique. For example in Ashes and Blood by Woodkid below, the chorus part was sung in Shuriman which is a fictional language developed for League of Legends lore.

Avatar The Last Airbender

ATLA is a famous childhood series that almost everyone my age watched. Aang, an avatar of four elements who was the only survivor of Air Nomad have to fulfill his destiny. Along with his friends Katara, Sokka and Toph, they strive to end the Fire Nation's war. The ending was a satisfaction where Aang was taking Fire Lord Ozai's power away rather than just killing him.

I started binge watching this during pandemic because I could not remember the story line since the last time I watched it. Honestly, the story line and animation, the application of diverse culture in fighting movement, the small details like way of greeting and hairstyles is fantastic. All of it was intriguing, awesome and captivating with its comedy element and conflict. ATLA got 9.3/10 average on IMDB with the lowest being 6.8.

Stranger Things

This horror television series was first aired in 2016, with the last season being in 2025. A group of young friends witnessed a supernatural forces that was related to the government exploitations. Over time, the town called Hawkins has seen various unexplained events that was actually caused by a different dimension, The Upside Down.

The first to three season was good, where it only focuses on Eleven's ability and the small town mystery. However, Vecna and a lot of side characters started appear which makes it a bit pack. The ending season could have been better since there are some plot holes and scenes that were unexplainable in the series. The hype for the last season was huge in social media where me and Fariha were also excited to watch it. Unfortunately, it did not meet the audiences' expectations which makes everyone feels disappointed.

Ready Player One

This film was about a virtual world called OASIS where normal people in the advanced world live. They can roleplaying, racing, dancing and many more in that reality. A challenge was left for the users when the creator died and whoever succeed gets a total control over OASIS. As the story goes, the characters started to realize and collecting clues of the challenge, even when the clue being the smallest thing to realize.

I was so amazed when I watched this movie for the first time. The detailing in future world and transformation was incredibly good. I like the way these characters realizing things that fill in the puzzle of questions and how are they teaming up to defeat the rival corporation IOI. Hence, this movie inspired me to play games and build a world together with my siblings where we all had a code name even they had already asked me a long time before that.
