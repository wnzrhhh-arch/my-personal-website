I play games like Roblox, Heartopia, Zoo Island, Cryptogram Letters, Unsolved Mysteries and Word Search. Despite having fun, I can learn how to solve problems and thinking out of box, especially in Unsolved Mysteries which is a detective game and Cryptogram Letters.
