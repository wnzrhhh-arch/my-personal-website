Reading

I start realising that I love reading at the age of 9 where I will read almost every reading materials that were available at my home including magazines, newspapers and even encyclopedia. In the meantime, I often borrowed books from library and friends which most of it were fantastic, mind-blowing and jaw dropping. Then when I was around 11 or 12 years old, my dad gift me a novel and that is when my habits of collecting books started.

I love genre like fantasy, mystery, sci-fi and thriller because I like to wonder and always thinking while reading. Besides, I also read stories from ebook and Wattpad and purchasing books whenever I feel like it. On top of that, I have several collections of books which mostly came from the similar genre. Some of it were about solving cases, magic, poetry, actions, astronomy and many more.
