Fellowship

Ever since I was a kid until now, I have go through three stages of education which also gave me three different group of friends. One from primary school, one from highschool and the last one being my university friends. Alhamdulillah, we were all still contacting each other and sometimes do an activity together.

Primary school friends

These are the people that I met at the age of 7 and still constantly meeting each other until this second. Every year on Hari Raya Aidilfitri, we will go to everybodys' houses since we live in the same area to visiting, talking and sometimes playing firecrackers and fireworks. This will be the moment that our stomach is full at the end of the day.

Interesting information: We have three sets of twins in this friend group which is twins, Irish twins and triplets. Our class was kind of known back in the day among the teachers because of this matter. They are always amaze to see us that we were oftenly told to take pictures together. However, the first brother from the triplets have passed away in 2025. Al-Fatihah to Mohd Zaini bin Ibrahim.

High school friends

My high school friends are the same people I befriend since Form 1 because our school is not huge with small amount of student that makes us left with one class for each form. Since we live at the hostel, we usually celebrated birthday of each other and taking photos every time we see a camera because we love taking picture so much. Our Telegram chat group was literally filled with pictures that it reach 5,000 and more images. Actually, I am glad we did that because I can look back at those pictures as it contains memories that cannot be played back. What is more, all of us have continued our study to different places that makes us rarely meet except for some. One of my male classmate have left this world on August 2025 and all that pictures are what we have left of him. Al-Fatihah to Abdul Hakim bin Zakaria.

University friends

These are my friends that I met after entering UiTM Kedah, also known as my classmates. I was scared to get along with them at first but it improved over time and I am glad it happened. We have done a lot of side quest together and exploring other regions whenever we could while spending our youth in a cheerful surrounding. We always have a good laugh and sometimes dumping random facts as a daily conversation which I will miss once we are not together anymore.
